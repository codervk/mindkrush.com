About MindKrush

MindKrush is founded in 2015 by passionate brothers Vijayakrishna and Balaajee. The founders are engineering graduates and had 8 years of industry exprience before entering into their own enterprenuership. So, they know the nuts and bolts of what they do.

Vijayakrishna is a graduate from MIT, Anna Univ. He has worked in research projects, core engineering projects, vehicle tracking software, smartphone games, mobile apps, web apps. He has been to Germany to get trained in test suites. He has constant passion in learning new technologies. He has worked on Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality applications.

While Vijayakrishna is the left brain, Balaajee is the right brain of MindKrush. He is artistic from the childhood. He thinks out of the box. He has worked in workflow softwares. He has worked in Australia for three years. He has worked with Infosys and Wipro. While all these gave a good industry experience, His passion drove him to design mobile game. Since then, he climbed the ladder and reached as Creative Head of MindKrush.
